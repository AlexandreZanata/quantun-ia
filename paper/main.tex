\begin{abstract}
We present quantun-ia, an open-source benchmark suite comparing variational quantum
circuits against parameter-matched classical baselines on synthetic and real datasets.
All experiments follow hypothesis-first discipline with multi-seed holdout evaluation,
bootstrap confidence intervals, and Holm-Bonferroni corrected comparisons.
\end{abstract}
