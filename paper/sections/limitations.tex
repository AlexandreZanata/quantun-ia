\section{Limitations}
\label{sec:limitations}

Our study has several scope boundaries that reviewers should weigh when interpreting claims.

\begin{itemize}
  \item \textbf{Simulator-only circuits.} All quantum results use PennyLane simulators
        (\texttt{default.qubit} / \texttt{lightning.qubit}); hardware noise and
        shot-based readout are not modeled.
  \item \textbf{Small qubit budgets.} Circuits use at most eight qubits and shallow
        depth to avoid barren-plateau regimes; results may not transfer to larger VQCs.
  \item \textbf{Binary classification focus.} Benchmarks are limited to two-class tasks
        on tabular, PCA-reduced MNIST, and low-dimensional synthetic data.
  \item \textbf{Classical parity, not SOTA.} Baselines are parameter-matched perceptrons
        and MLPs, not tuned deep networks or kernel SVMs from the literature.
  \item \textbf{Single-lab replication.} Metrics reported here come from one codebase
        and one compute environment; independent replication is encouraged via
        \texttt{make repro} and the Zenodo release bundle.
\end{itemize}

Future work includes noise-aware backends, hardware validation of exp\_021/022 claims,
and external leaderboard replication (MicroQML Bench).
